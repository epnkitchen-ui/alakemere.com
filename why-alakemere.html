Why businesses choose
Alakemere.

There is no shortage of consultants in Nigeria. What is in short supply is consultants who actually know the data, stay until the work lands, and measure themselves against your results — not their own outputs.

Reason 01

We know this market

Nigerian regulatory requirements, FIRS 2024 tax bands, infrastructure constraints, pension obligations, NDPR compliance — we understand the operating environment your business actually exists in. Every recommendation we make is calibrated to the Nigerian context, not adapted from a Western playbook.

Reason 02

Outcomes, not reports

We scope every engagement around measurable business results. Before work begins, we agree what success looks like — faster operations, better decisions, reduced compliance risk, more revenue. We measure ourselves against those outcomes. The deliverable is the change, not the document describing it.

Reason 03

We stay until it works

Most consultants deliver a report and disappear. We configure, train, iterate, and support until the solution is embedded in how your business actually operates. A system that isn't used is money wasted. We make sure it gets used.

See the difference for yourself.

A 30-minute call costs nothing. Book yours and let's talk about your business specifically.
