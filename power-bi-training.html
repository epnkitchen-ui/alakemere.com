Power BI training built on
your data, your questions.

Most Power BI training teaches you what the software can do. We teach your team what it can do for your specific business — connecting your real data sources, answering your actual reporting questions, and producing dashboards your leadership will use.

What the Programme Covers

From your data sources to
executive-grade dashboards.

Our Power BI training is built entirely around your organisation's data environment and reporting needs. We conduct a discovery session before designing the curriculum — ensuring every exercise is relevant and every output is directly usable.

Data Source Connection

We train your team to connect Power BI to your actual data sources — whether that is Excel files, SQL databases, SharePoint, or cloud platforms — and understand how to structure data for analysis.

Data Modelling

We cover data modelling fundamentals — relationships, star schemas, calculated columns — using your organisation's real data structure as the teaching example.

DAX Measures

We teach the specific DAX measures your dashboards need — not a library of formulas, but the specific calculations that answer the business questions your leadership team actually asks.

Dashboard Design

We train your team to design dashboards that communicate clearly — choosing the right visualisations, applying your brand, and structuring layouts for executive consumption.

Report Distribution & Governance

We cover Power BI Service — publishing, sharing, workspace management, and row-level security — so your team can deploy reports to the right people with appropriate access controls.

PL-300 Preparation

For participants pursuing certification, we align the programme to the PL-300 exam curriculum and provide exam preparation support.

What Participants Gain
Working Power BI dashboards built on their own business data
Ability to build and maintain new reports independently
Connection of existing data sources to a live reporting environment
PL-300 exam readiness for certification candidates

your actual business questions.
