Alakemere is a management consulting firm founded in Abuja and built specifically for the Nigerian operating environment. We combine deep analytical expertise with an intimate understanding of how business actually works here.

Our Story

A firm built from
the ground up.

"The businesses winning in Nigeria today are not working harder — they are working with better information, clearer systems, and sharper decisions."

Alakemere's name carries heritage — Ala, the Igbo word for earth, fused with our founder's family name, Odinkemere. That ground-rootedness is not decoration. It shapes how we engage: with honesty, specificity, and the directness of someone who has earned the right to be in the room.

We exist to close the gap between where Nigerian businesses are and where they are capable of being. We do that through data-driven advisory, rigorous process improvement, and the kind of ongoing partnership that most consulting firms don't offer — because they've moved on to the next client before the work has landed.

— Founder, Alakemere

Our Values

Three principles.
No exceptions.

01
Evidence over assertion

Every recommendation we make is grounded in data, analysis, and verified outcomes. We do not sell confidence we cannot back. "We are the best" means nothing. "Here is what we found in your data, and here is what it means for your Q3" means everything.

02
Outcomes over reports

Our engagements are scoped around measurable business results — faster operations, better decisions, reduced compliance risk. We measure ourselves against those outcomes, not the volume of documents we produce. The deliverable is the change.

03
Partnership over transaction

We stay long enough to see the work land — configuring, training, iterating, supporting. Nigerian business owners have seen too many consultants who disappear after the invoice. We do not operate that way. The relationship does not end when the project does.
