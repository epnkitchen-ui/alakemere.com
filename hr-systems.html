HR systems that fit how
your business actually works.

Most Nigerian businesses are managing HR in spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, or generic software that was not built for their operating environment. The cost — in time, errors, and compliance risk — is significant and largely invisible.

What We Deliver

A configured, working HR system
your team will actually use.

We select, configure, and deploy HR platforms suited to your organisation's size, structure, and Nigerian compliance requirements — including our own Nara platform for SMEs. We do not just install software. We configure it to your workflows, train your team, and stay until adoption is real.

HR Systems Needs Assessment

We assess your current HR processes, team size, budget, and compliance requirements to identify the right platform — whether that is our Nara product, a third-party system, or a hybrid approach.

Platform Configuration

We configure the selected platform to your organisation's specific structure — departments, approval hierarchies, leave policies, payroll rules, and reporting requirements.

Data Migration

We migrate existing employee records, payroll history, and HR data into the new system — cleaning and validating data as part of the migration process.

Team Training

We train your HR team and line managers to use the system effectively — covering daily operations, payroll processing, reporting, and system administration.

Hypercare & Support

We provide a defined hypercare period after go-live — available to resolve issues, answer questions, and refine configuration as your team encounters real-world scenarios.

What You Gain
A working HR system configured for your specific business
Nigerian compliance built into your payroll from day one
Clean, reliable employee data in one place
A team that knows how to use the system properly
HR systems that work for
the Nigerian operating environment.
