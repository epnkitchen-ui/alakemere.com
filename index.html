Who We Are

Rooted in Nigeria.
Built on evidence.

"The businesses winning in Nigeria today are not working harder — they are working with better information, clearer systems, and sharper decisions."

Alakemere is a management consulting firm founded in Abuja and built specifically for the Nigerian operating environment. Our name carries heritage — Ala, the Igbo word for earth, fused with our founder's family name, Odinkemere. That ground-rootedness shapes how we engage: with honesty, specificity, and the directness of someone who has earned the right to be in the room.

01
Evidence over assertion
Every recommendation is grounded in data, analysis, and verified outcomes — not theory or trends. We back what we say.
02
Outcomes over reports
Our engagements are scoped around measurable business results. We measure ourselves against those outcomes — not the volume of documents we produce.
03
Partnership over transaction
We stay long enough to see the work land — configuring, training, iterating, supporting. The relationship does not end when the invoice is paid.

Auditing Services

Bad data costs more
than you think.
We fix that.

Poor data quality, broken reporting processes, and unreviewed compliance gaps are silent drains on business performance. Our auditing practice finds them, quantifies them, and gives you a clear path to resolution.

Data Quality Audits

We assess the accuracy, completeness, and consistency of your data across systems — identifying where information breaks down and how to establish a trustworthy source of truth for leadership decision-making.

Process & Compliance Audits

We review existing processes against regulatory requirements (CCP, GMCP, Nigerian compliance frameworks) and internal policies — identifying gaps and recommending corrective actions that are practical, not theoretical.

Operational Risk Assessment

We map your operational risk landscape — from data handling to supply chain dependencies — and produce a prioritised risk register with mitigation strategies your leadership can act on immediately.

Reporting Lifecycle Review

We audit the end-to-end reporting chain — from data capture to executive presentation — identifying delays and inaccuracies, then redesigning workflows using Power Automate and BI tools to optimise the full cycle.

ERP Systems Audit

For organisations already running ERP platforms, we assess system utilisation, data integrity, user adoption, and integration gaps — providing a structured remediation roadmap to maximise your existing investment.

Dashboard & Analytics Audit

We review existing Power BI or Excel reporting environments — assessing whether the right questions are being asked, whether data sources are reliable, and whether outputs are genuinely driving decisions.

HR Consulting

Your people data should work
as hard as your people do.

Effective HR is not just about policies and payroll. It is about building the data systems, processes, and structures that let your organisation manage people with the same rigour it applies to finance or operations. Alakemere's HR practice bridges that gap.

01
HR Systems Implementation
We select, configure, and deploy HR platforms suited to your organisation's size, structure, and Nigerian compliance requirements — including our own Nara platform for SMEs.
02
Payroll Structure & Compliance
We design payroll frameworks compliant with Nigerian tax law (PAYE, FIRS 2024 bands), pension obligations, NHF, NSITF, and ITF — and build the systems to run them accurately every cycle.
03
Workforce Data Management
We build integrated people databases, onboarding workflows, and employee data pipelines — ensuring your HR records are accurate, accessible, and useful for workforce planning.
04
People Analytics & Reporting
From headcount dashboards to attrition analysis and performance tracking, we build HR reporting that gives leadership the visibility needed to make better people decisions.
